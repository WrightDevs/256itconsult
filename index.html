About 265 IT Consult

Established in Eastern Europe in 2001, 265 IT Consult has grown into a leading UK-based IT infrastructure solutions company. With over two decades of experience, we've been at the forefront of technological advancements, helping businesses of all sizes navigate the complex world of IT.

Our team of expert consultants and technicians are dedicated to delivering tailored solutions that drive efficiency, security, and growth for our clients.
